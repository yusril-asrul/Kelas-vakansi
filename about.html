Our Story

In 2019, we, as members of 3 (three) people, founded Vakansi Class and are determined to become a capable travel agent for local and foreign tourists

After careful preparation, we will begin to develop vacation classes in 2022. As a form of our commitment to becoming a trusted travel agent, we established a Vaansi Class office in the city of Kendari. Not only that, we are also starting to collaborate with the government and travel agents to expand our market share.

With the support of travel guests and public trust, we are very confident of being able to provide the best service on every trip. We deploy a professional team and design a pleasant trip in an efficient time
